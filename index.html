A Fridge That Grabs your items for you

After a long day at work, nobody wants to waste energy getting out of their comfortable spot to enjoy a cold beverage. What if a robot does this for you.

  • Accessible

    Useful for the elderly and anyone with mobility issues. This saves them from making unnecessary trips to the kitchen if it is something that causes discomfort.

  • Voice Activated

    When called, the Movable Fridge would pop out of its hole in the wall and scoot across the floor without bumping into any furniture or pets.

  • Don't Miss Anything

    Users would be able to grab items from the fridge without missing an important play or interrupting a conversation with friends

  • LIDAR and Depth Sensors

    Using built-in light detection and ranging (lidar), as well as a depth sensor, it would be able to scan a household so it can navigate the area with ease.
